About the Gallery

Founded in 2018, ArtVista is a contemporary art space dedicated to showcasing emerging and mid-career artists from around the globe. Our mission is to bridge the gap between creators and collectors, fostering a deeper appreciation for visual storytelling.

With three exhibition halls and a rotating calendar of avant-garde installations, we invite you to discover works that challenge, inspire, and transform. Our permanent collection includes over 200 pieces spanning painting, sculpture, photography, and new media.

Located in the heart of the Arts District — open Tuesday to Sunday.
